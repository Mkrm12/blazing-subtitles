The pipeline for extracting Chinese audio and OCR is working well.

[TECHNICAL HURDLES]
We hit a massive error with Episode 10 being physically corrupted.
OpenCV kept crashing, even though VLC media player played it perfectly.
VLC was just hiding the broken frames and smoothing over the errors.
We had to change the formatting of the episode to ensure it was read.
Used an ffmpeg 'car wash' command to completely rebuild the video index.
After the wash, the pipeline swept the episode perfectly without crashing.
