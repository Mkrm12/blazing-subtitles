DATE: 2026-04-29
STATUS: 40 Episodes Completed (100% Translation Milestone)

[PIPELINE & TOOLING PROGRESS]
All 40 episodes are finally translated! A massive accomplishment and an incredible feeling.
Built a "Florence Cleaner" script for Episode 30 onwards. It surgically cuts the Florence-2 visual descriptions down to a single line. The massive paragraphs were eating up memory, lengthening wait times, and providing useless context. The cleaner fixed this instantly.
One major retrospective takeaway: batch-scraping all remaining episodes around Ep 20 saved time, but it killed the iterative pipeline. I couldn't make micro-improvements to the extraction script for the later episodes.

[LLM SYNERGY & TRANSLATION]
Encountered serious memory limits with DeepSeek. During Ep 28, the chat context maxed out, and switching to a new chat caused a noticeable drop in translation quality for Part 2.
Pivoted to a dual-LLM tag-team approach: DeepSeek + Gemini.
DeepSeek handles the blunt, highly accurate structural translation, and Gemini does the rewording. Gemini smooths out the flow beautifully—it can be a bit dramatic sometimes, but it yielded a massive improvement in writing quality.
The final 3 tournament episodes had intense on-screen text and signs that heavily interfered with the pipeline. The pipeline couldn't filter it natively, but the DeepSeek + Gemini combo managed to power through and filter the noise.

[RETROSPECTIVE & NEXT STEPS]
Because of the evolving pipeline and my growing understanding of the show, the later episodes are noticeably higher quality than the earlier ones.
Realized I didn't run stitcher.py on some of the early episodes, meaning there are almost certainly raw timestamp clashes and errors hiding in them.
The next 7 days will be pure refinement and deployment prep.
Need to conduct deep research on all character names, catchphrases, and yo-yo move names.
Must do a global consistency sweep to fix move names across all 40 files and append official episode numbers.
